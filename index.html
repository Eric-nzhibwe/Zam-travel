Featured Destinations



Kafue national park

The kafue national is located in western Zambia,it covers about 22,400km - making it one of the largest national parks in africa.
Roughly a 4-5 hour drive from Lusaka(depending on which gate you use).It is famous for the big cats,rare antelopes and wild dogs.
include: Game drives(day and night),walking safaris and boat safaris. A 4x4 vehicle is strongly recommended

Shiwa Ng'andu Estate

Shiwa Ng'andu means "Lake of the royal crocodiles"in the local bemba language.
This estate is owned by Gore Brown's descendants,it also includes working farms,hot springs and nearby wildlife areas.
The manor includes wildlife viewing,guided tours of the manor(full of historical photographs and books),fishing and hicking.

Its route is accessible by road(aboyt 55km,but some parts are rough).A 4x4 vehicle is recommended,especially in rain season.
