About Zam-Travel

Our Story

Zam-Travel is a premier travel agency dedicated to showcasing the breathtaking beauty and rich culture of Zambia. Founded in 2010, our mission is to provide unforgettable travel experiences, from the majestic Victoria Falls to the vibrant markets of Lusaka, and and many more.

Our Mission

  • Create personalized travel experiences that highlight Zambia's natural wonders.
  • Promote sustainable tourism to preserve our environment and support local communities.
  • Offer exceptional customer service to ensure seamless and memorable journeys.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert guides with deep knowledge of Zambia.
  • Tailored itineraries for every traveler.
  • Commitment to eco-friendly and community-focused travel.
